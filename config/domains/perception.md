# Domain: Perception & psychophysics  (anchor)

**Typical constructs / measures:** detection & discrimination thresholds, just-noticeable differences,
perceptual scaling, category boundaries, and mental representations / perceptual priors.

**Typical paradigms (affinity):** GSP / MCMCP (representation recovery), staircase / dense AXB /
same-different (thresholds), static parametric designs (function mapping).

**Common confounds / controls:** response bias vs. sensitivity (use signal-detection measures);
adaptation & order effects (randomize / counterbalance); range & anchoring effects; display / audio
calibration (use prescreens); sustained attention.

**Effect sizes:** within-subject perceptual effects are often large and reliable, but threshold
estimates need enough trials per participant to be stable — feed this into the power analysis.

**PsyNet prescreens to consider:** `HeadphoneTest` / `AntiphaseHeadphoneTest` / `HugginsHeadphoneTest`
(audio), `ColorBlindnessTest` / `ColorVocabularyTest` (color), `AttentionTest`.
